# Privacy Policy — RMP for CUNY Schedule Builder

**Last updated: April 19, 2026**

## What this extension does

RMP for CUNY Schedule Builder reads instructor names from CUNY Schedule Builder pages and displays Rate My Professors ratings next to those names.

## Data sent to external services

- **Rate My Professors** (`ratemyprofessors.com`): The extension sends instructor names and your selected CUNY campus to the Rate My Professors public GraphQL API to fetch ratings and reviews. No other data is sent.

No data is sent to any other server. No analytics, no telemetry, no third-party services.

## Data stored locally

All data is stored in `chrome.storage.local` on your device only:

- Your selected CUNY campus
- Cached school IDs, professor ratings, and top reviews (to avoid redundant network requests)

Nothing is synced to any server controlled by this extension.

## What this extension does NOT do

- No analytics
- No telemetry
- No advertising
- No tracking
- No account or sign-up
- No AI processing or third-party API calls
- No collection of personal information
- No modification of CUNY Schedule Builder data

## Deleting your data

Click "Clear cache" in the extension popup to wipe all cached data. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

## Contact

If you have questions, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
